# Metrics Definitions

## Activation Rate
**Formula**: `(Unique Users who performed "campaign_create") / (Total Signups)`
- **Explanation**: The percentage of users who have performed the core value action of the platform.

## MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
**Formula**: `Sum(Price of all Active Subscriptions in Month M)`
- **Explanation**: The predictable revenue generated by the business every month. Does not include one-time fees.

## Churn Rate (Monthly)
**Formula**: `(Users who left in Month M) / (Users active at Start of Month M)`
- **Explanation**: The percentage of customers who cancel their subscription within a given month.

## ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
**Formula**: `Total MRR / Total Active Paid Users`
- **Explanation**: The average amount of revenue generated per paid user.

## LTV (Lifetime Value)
**Formula**: `ARPU / Churn Rate` (Simplified) or `Sum(Revenue from Cohort) / Users in Cohort`
- **Explanation**: The total revenue a business can expect from a single customer account throughout their lifecycle.

## Cohort Retention
**Formula**: `(Users from Cohort C active in Month M) / (Total Users in Cohort C)`
- **Explanation**: Shows what percentage of users from a specific signup month are still active over time.
